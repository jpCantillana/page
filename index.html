About Me

I am a researcher at the Department of Business Decisions and Analytics, University of Vienna, where I have been working for the past year and a half. My current project involves developing machine learning-based acceleration techniques for routing problems. My research interests include exact and heuristic methods, logistics, and mathematical modeling.
